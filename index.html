KEY PORTION ASSUMPTIONS
  • Beef (Type O): 10 lb purchased (8 lb eaten, 2 lb buffer). 1 lb 70/30 ground per dinner.
  • Chicken: 16 lb purchased. Paul 14 oz, Addie 10 oz per dinner.
  • Salmon (Mutually Beneficial): 10 lb box (8oz portions). 2 days/week post-workout. Perfect for both Type O and Type A.
  • Eggs: 72 eggs/month. 6 per workout day.
  • Cooking Fat: Moderate Organic Olive Oil/Beef Tallow/Ghee/Coconut Oil.
VEGETABLE PLAN SUMMARY

16 dinners per month structure emphasizing nutrient-dense options:

  • Broccoli: 8 lbs/mo (Blanch then sauté with garlic)
  • Zucchini: 4 lbs/mo (Sautéed quarter moons)
  • Leeks: 2 lb batch covers 2 dinners (Cheese sauce/roux)
  • Kabocha Squash: 5 lb squash covers 2 dinners
  • Spinach: 2 lbs/month (primarily for post-workout eggs)

On Sale Rotations: Mix things up with Green Beans, Asparagus, Swiss Chard, Spaghetti Squash, Romaine, or Chayote when discounted.

TYPE-O: LOW-CARB DINNER IDEAS
  • Burger Bowls: Served over sautéed zucchini or broccoli.
  • Garlic Herb Beef: Ground beef sautéed with garlic, herbs, and green beans.
  • Cheesy Beef Patties: Beef patties paired with broccoli and a creamy leek cheese sauce.
  • Zucchini Beef Skillet: Ground beef cooked in a skillet with zucchini quarter moons.
  • Beef & Kabocha: Ground beef and kabocha squash plate, perfect for higher-carb days.
TYPE-A: EASY TOFU DINNER IDEAS

Tip: Use Extra-Firm for steaks & crispy frying, Firm for stir-fries, and Silken for blending into sauces.

  • Crispy Honey-Soy (Extra-Firm): Press, cube, toss in soy/garlic/ginger, pan-sear, glaze.
  • Broccoli Stir-Fry (Firm): Pan-fry cubes, toss with blanched broccoli & savory brown sauce.
  • Spicy Garlic Tofu (Firm): Seared tofu in a chili-garlic sauce over sautéed greens.
  • Baked Lemon-Herb (Extra-Firm): Marinate in lemon/olive oil/herbs, bake until firm.
  • Hearty Miso Soup (Silken): Cubed silken tofu simmered in a rich miso broth with spinach, leeks, and mushrooms.

POST-WORKOUT SHAKE
  • Batch Size: 32 oz (Split 16 oz Paul / 16 oz Addie)
  • Protein Powders (~64g total per batch):
    • Plant Protein: 2 scoops (34g protein)
    • Hydrolyzed Collagen: 2 scoops (20g protein)

  • Liquid Base & Add-ins:

ELECTROLYTES & FASTING
  • Daily 16/8 Protocol: Critical for hydration on Mon, Wed, Sat.
  • Quarterly Extended Fast: 4.5 days every 12 weeks. Starts Tuesday evening, ends Sunday afternoon.
  • Key Benefits of Fasting:
    • Autophagy: Cellular cleanup process that clears out damaged cells & toxins.
    • Stem Cell Creation: Deep fasting triggers immune system regeneration and new stem cell production.
    • Other Benefits: Insulin sensitivity reset, reduced inflammation, and mental clarity.
  • Electrolyte Recipe (per 32 oz water):
    • 1/4 tsp gray Celtic sea salt, 1/8 tsp potassium salt
    • 1/2 tsp True Lemon/Lime powder, 1/4 tsp Magnesium citrate powder
CORE COOKING FAT SYSTEM
  • Organic Olive Oil: Low/medium heat. Ideal for dressings, finishing, and light sautéing.
  • Beef Tallow: High heat. Excellent for searing steaks and roasting root vegetables.
  • Ghee: Medium/high heat. Great for eggs, curries, and general cooking with a buttery flavor.
  • Coconut Oil: Medium heat. Best for baking, curries, and tropical flavor profiles.

  • Salmon: Keep 8oz portions frozen individually.
  • Eggs: Buy 2 packs bi-weekly for optimal freshness.

Rules & Guides

  • Calorie Control: Adjust cooking fats and cheese portions first when cutting calories.
  • Sauce/Flavor: Leverage garlic, ginger, soy, and herbs to elevate basics without sugar.
  • Rotation Strategy: Rotate primary proteins weekly to prevent fatigue.
  • Macro Knobs: Use complex carbs (kabocha, sweet potato) for workout days, strictly low-carb otherwise.
  • Prep Checklist: Check freezer inventory monthly; restock bulk proteins on sale.
